Our History

Coffee is one of the most popular drinks in the world. It is also one of the most profitable beverages, with millions of cups consumed daily across the globe. The rich aroma and distinct taste make it a favorite for people of all ages. Coffee culture has evolved into a global phenomenon, with specialty coffee shops, artisanal roasts, and diverse brewing methods.

Interestingly, coffee was first discovered in 1880, when legend has it that an Ethiopian goat herder noticed his goats becoming unusually energetic after eating the berries of a certain tree. Curious, he tried the berries himself and experienced a newfound burst of energy.

The beans are sourced from around the world and are grown in artisanal coffee houses.Coffee is a popular drink in many countries.
